# Superstore Business Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales and returns data to uncover opportunities to improve profitability, optimize advertising spend, and reduce costly product returns. Using Tableau, I built an interactive dashboard to identify the products, categories, customers, and regions that most impact business performance.

The goal was to provide data-driven recommendations to help the company increase profits and avoid bankruptcy.

---

## Business Questions

- What are the biggest profit centers and largest loss-makers?
- Which products should be discontinued?
- Which subcategories should the company focus on?
- Where and when should advertising dollars be spent?
- Which products and customers have the highest return rates?
- How do returns affect profitability?

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Profit & Loss Analysis

Identified the top-performing and worst-performing combinations of region, subcategory, and products.

### 2. Product Optimization

Highlighted products and subcategories that should be discontinued or prioritized based on profit performance.

### 3. Advertising Opportunities

Analyzed monthly average profit by state to determine the best locations and times to invest in advertising.

### 4. Returns Analysis

Used returns data to identify products and customers with abnormal return rates and evaluate how returns reduce profitability.

---

## Key Insights

- A small number of products and regions drive most profits  
- Several products consistently lose money and should be removed  
- Specific states and months offer stronger advertising ROI  
- High-return products and customers negatively impact margins  
- Returns analysis can guide smarter inventory and customer policies
