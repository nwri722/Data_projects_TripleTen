# Venture Capital Investment Database Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes venture capital investment data to uncover trends in startup funding, investor activity, deal volume, and market opportunities. The goal was to use structured data analysis to understand where capital is flowing and what factors influence investment behavior.

---

## Business Questions

- Which industries receive the most funding?
- What regions attract the highest investment activity?
- Which investors are most active?
- How have investment trends changed over time?
- What patterns exist in startup funding rounds?

---

## Tools Used

- SQL  
- Spreadsheet Analysis  
- Data Cleaning  
- Database Querying  
- Data Visualization  

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains information related to:

- Startups / companies  
- Funding rounds  
- Investors  
- Investment amounts  
- Dates of transactions  
- Industries and locations  

---

## Analysis Performed

### Investment Trends

- Total funding over time  
- Number of deals by year/month  
- Average investment size  

### Industry Analysis

- Top-funded sectors  
- Fast-growing categories  
- Emerging markets  

### Investor Analysis

- Most active venture capital firms  
- Largest investors by total funding  
- Repeat investment behavior  

### Geographic Analysis

- Funding by country / region  
- Startup ecosystem hotspots  

---

## Key Insights

- Capital tends to concentrate in a small number of sectors  
- Certain regions dominate startup investment activity  
- Repeat investors often focus on specific industries  
- Funding patterns shift based on market conditions
